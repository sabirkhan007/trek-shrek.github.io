Buran Ghati Trek: An Adventure Lover's Paradise

Assuming you are a sharp admirer of adventure, we have curated the best adventurous camp for you: the Buran Ghati Trek (15,000 Ft. Elevation and 26 Km Distance). This trek is particularly adored by thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts. The Buran Ghati trek is brimming with excitement, stunning vistas, thrilling experiences, and much more. Witness the rich, breathtaking meadows of Dayara and snow-covered mountains as far as the eye can see. With diverse landscapes, this destination offers an unforgettable journey.

Situated at an altitude of 15,000 ft in Himachal Pradesh, the Buran Ghati trek is considered one of the best in the state. Spanning a distance of approximately 25-30 km, it lies within the Greater Himalayas region. The journey begins at Janglik and concludes at Barua. One of the most thrilling moments of the trek is when trekkers reach the summit of Buran Ghati and rappel down the other side—a truly exhilarating experience. At this altitude, snow blankets the region, enhancing the trek’s beauty.

The villages of Janglik and Barua, which you’ll pass through, are beautifully designed and offer a glimpse into the rich culture of Himachal Pradesh. From the summit, trekkers are treated to breathtaking views of the Dhauladhar range and glimpses of many other peaks in the vicinity. The magnificent sight of the Kinner Kailash peak from Buran Ghati is a must-see highlight of the trek. Our expertise in the region ensures we offer the best Buran Ghati trek package for an unforgettable adventure.
