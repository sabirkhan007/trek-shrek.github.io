Overview

Region: Uttarakhand

Trek Duration: 04 Days

Trek Difficulty: Easy to moderate, with some steeps now and then

Max Altitude: Approximately 11,500 ft.

Dayara Bugyal Trek Distance: 17 Km

Temperature: 13°C to 18°C during the day and -3°C to 4°C during the night

Best Time: November to January, March to Mid-May

Start / End Point of the Trek: Raithal, Uttarakhand

ATM: Last ATM available in Dehradun

Perched at an altitude of around 11,500 ft - Dayara Bugyal is considered as India’s one of the most beautiful alpine meadows trek. Located in the North-west part of Uttarakhand, this garden of nature can make anyone go weak on their knees. "Bugyal” means meadow in the regional language, which means land covered with wildflowers. It offers 270-degree views of the mesmerizing snow-clad mountains and valleys holding the blanket of nature. On your way, you will be mesmerized with myriad hues of nature changing colors with every season to towering peaks like Gangotri, Draupadi ka Danda. It is a place filled with high biodiversity with picturesque views.

04 Days / 03 Nights
6-15 persons
Trekking distance : 17 Km

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Dehradun to Raithal

Day 2: Raithal to Gui, 5 Km Trek

Day 3: The Summit Day, 7 Km trek

Day 4: Gui to Dehradun

Detail Itinerary

Day 1: Dehradun to Raithal
  • Pick up from Dehradun Railway station at 7:30 a.m. Dehradun is well connected with Delhi through railway and road routes. Overnight buses and trains are available.
  • Meet at Dehradun. Drive from Dehradun to Raithal.
  • Drive to Raithal via Uttarkashi.
  • Arrive at Raithal village, a small village located 20 km above the city of Uttarkashi, which serves as the starting point of the trek. Stay at the homestay in Raithal.
  • A quick introduction & briefing session with the trek leader and fellow travelers.
  • Dinner and rest at the homestay.
Day 2: Raithal to Gui, 5 Km Trek
  • Excursion to Gui Campsite from Raithal after breakfast.
  • A 5 km ascend trek to Gui campsite of easy grade passing through the oak forest.
  • Arrival at the Gui campsite, hot lunch at the campsite.
  • An evening acclimatization walk.
  • Evening refreshments and snacks post-walk.
  • Overnight stay at the camps.
Day 3: The Summit Day, 7 Km Trek
  • 3.5 Km trek to Dayara Bugyal from the campsite after breakfast.
  • Rest and packed lunch at the Dayara Viewpoint.
  • Descend back to Gui Campsite from Dayara Top.
  • Arrival at the campsite. Dinner at the campsite.
Day 4: Gui to Dehradun
  • Descend back to Raithal village from Gui after breakfast on a 5 km trek.
  • A 235 km drive back to Dehradun from Raithal village, passing through Uttarkashi.
  • Lunch and evening snacks will be provided at roadside inns during transport.
  • The expected time of arrival in Dehradun is between 7:30 PM and 8:00 PM. You can plan your onward travel accordingly.

More Details about Dayara Bugyal Trek

  • Please provide a valid ID at the time of check-in. The exact location will be shared with you post-booking.
  • The trek takes place through an eco-friendly zone; do not encourage littering at local sites or campsites. Travelers littering may be fined.
  • Water is very precious, so conservation is appreciated.
  • Neat and clean vehicle for the journey with experienced drivers, especially for hilly terrains, is available.
  • Carry dry snacks/food from home (you will save a lot of money). Carry water bottles with you so that you can refill them.
  • Keep your luggage to a minimum; the more you carry, the more hassle it will be.
  • All activities depend upon the weather.
  • If the trek needs to be canceled, the authorities should be informed beforehand.
  • It is recommended to carry enough warm clothes and the right kind of shoes for the Dayara Bugyal trek.
  • Children below the age of 7 years are not recommended for the Dayara Bugyal trek.
