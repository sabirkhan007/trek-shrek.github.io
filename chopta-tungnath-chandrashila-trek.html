Overview

Region: Uttarakhand

Trek Duration: 04 Days

Trek Difficulty: Easy To Moderate

Route: Delhi - Rishikesh – Devprayag - Joshimath – Auli – Sari – Chopta – Tungnath – Chandrashila – Chopta – Sari – Deoriatal – Rishikesh - Delhi

Auli is a Himalayan ski resort and hill station in the north Indian state of Uttarakhand. It’s surrounded by coniferous and oak forests, plus the Nanda Devi and Nar Parvat mountains. A long cable car links Auli to the town of Joshimath. North of Auli are the colorful Badrinath Temple, a Hindu pilgrimage site, and the Valley of FlowersNational Park, with its alpine flora and wildlife like snow leopards and red foxes.

04 Days / 03 Nights
6-15 persons
Trekking distance : 62 Km

Short Itinerary

DAY 0: Delhi to Rishikesh

Distance: 238 km

DAY 1: Rishikesh to Joshimath

Distance: 249 km

DAY 2: Joshimath - Auli - Chopta

DAY 3: Chopta - Tungnath - Chandrashila to Sari

DAY 4: Deoriatal - Rishikesh - Delhi

Detail Itinerary

Day 1: Rishikesh to Joshimath

On arrival at Rishikesh, continue your journey to Joshimath. On the way, halt at Devprayag Sangam. Reach Joshimath by evening, which is the base camp of Auli. Check-in to your hotel. Freshen up and visit Narsingh Temple. Overnight stay at Joshimath.

Day 2: Joshimath - Auli - Chopta

After breakfast at 7:00 a.m, check out from the hotel and head to Auli (approximately 1 hour drive). Enjoy and explore Auli. Take a cable car ride for stunning views of the Himalayas. Enjoy the snow activities. Come back to your vehicle at 1:00 p.m and have lunch in Joshimath. Depart at 2:00 p.m to Chopta for overnight stay at camps (4-5 hrs drive).

Day 3: Chopta-Tungnath-Chandrashila to Sari

Have breakfast in the morning at 7:00 a.m and start early for the Chopta-Tungnath-Chandrashila trek. Reach Chopta and begin the trek to Tungnath. Visit Tungnath Temple (the highest Shiva temple in the world). Continue the trek to Chandrashila Peak for panoramic views. Descend back to Chopta and drive to Sari. Check-in to your accommodation in Sari.

Day 4: Deoriatal-Rishikesh-Delhi

Wake up early in the morning at 6:00 a.m. Have breakfast at 7:00 a.m., then start your trek of 2km to Deoria Tal from Sari. Descend back at 9:00 a.m. Refresh, and at 10:00 a.m., check out from the hotel. Start your journey back to Rishikesh (approximately 8-9 hours drive). During the journey, take a halt at Dhari Devi temple and visit the beautiful temple built on the Alaknanda river. Arrive in Rishikesh at 6:00 p.m. and continue your journey to Delhi.
